Photographic Print

Utilizing the photographic medium, Yamaguchi sought to convey the contrast between safety and uneasiness by portraying the comfort of the sunset in the setting while adding subtle, translucent figures in the center of the frame to startle the audience when seen. Taken in the Japanese Tea Garden located in the heart of Golden Gate Park, the artist retold the story of Spirited Away, by Hayao Miyazaki when spirits of the spirit world started to manifest during the transition from day to night. This depiction highlights the tension and the impending danger of the scene, and poses as a stark contrast to the natural setting. The spirits and the komorebi in the photograph underscores the tranquil, mystical, yet perilous nature of the spirit world.
